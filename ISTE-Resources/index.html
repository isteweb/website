About ISTE

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is the leading National Professional non-profit making Society for the Technical Education System in our country with the motto of Career Development of Teachers and Personality Development of Students and overall development of our Technical Education System.

Our Mission

ISTE inspires educators worldwide to use technology to innovate teaching and learning, accelerate good practice and solve tough problems in education by providing community, knowledge and the ISTE Standards, a framework for rethinking education and empowering learners.

Our Vision

ISTE’s vision is that all educators are empowered to harness technology to accelerate innovation in teaching and learning, and inspire learners to reach their greatest potential.

A blend of events, workshops and lectures is what formed the groundbreaking 17th convention, Atreya'19 which was conducted by ISTE NITC Students' chapter. It witnessed a huge footfall of more than 2.5k people coming together from all over the state. The programme commenced with an inaugural ceremony on 9th February, the chief guest being C R Thomas, the Deputy director of VSSC, a research center of the ISRO. Lectures of eminent personalities were held and a large variety of workshops were conducted. An array of vents like CSI, Dream Codex, Admania to name a few, were present. The convention also consisted of a performance of the band Second Class Citizens. Atreya'19 concluded on a successful note.

Spreading knowledge and awareness through technical events is a cherished goal of ISTE. Keeping this in mind, annually, School Spectrum is conducted with enlightening and fun events that test various skills of students. There are numerous activities to please any palate. Students are given an opportunity to put forth their oratory skills through events such as Beat The Talk, acting skills through Tech Charades, analytical skills through CSI, or marketing abilities through Admania. The Spectrum is the pride of the ISTE as it allows the club to reach out to the students and put up activities that not only allows them to show their expertise but also to help them realize their capability and nurture it.

"Education means igniting the lamp. Not just filling the buckets." The IIT colleges in India are the dream of every aspiring Indian engineer and scientist. Planning, preparation and practice are the main themes by which students shall be nurtured and are made to focus on the goal. So presenting you JEEnius to help boost the last leg of preparation of the students and set to ease their load. A systematic and purposeful selection of questions from fundamental to analytical skills, aptitude, comprehension, alertness, approach in various aspects of the subject and time management. Well-planned and well coordinated effort with clear understanding of the basic concepts and persistent practice help make the learner confident in subjects and competent in succeeding. Every student shall try and achieve, the much needed things are : dream, goal setting, plan, effort and above all passion. Hoping the best for you!!!
